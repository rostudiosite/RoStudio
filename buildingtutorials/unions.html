Roblox Studio Unions, what are they and how could we use them?

In this tutorial we will be explaining everything about unions and how you can use them.

What are unions?

You might be surprised at how simple it is. A union is just multiple parts merged into one.

How could you use them?

You can use them for almost everything, you should only use them if you need to. Using unions, you can make multiple shapes and objects without using meshes. If you are working with a model full of parts, this would make your workspace much more organized.

You don't only have to use them for merging parts together! They have multiple other purposes!

Downsides?

Sadly, almost everything has a downside; unions aren't that lag-efficient. When looking at a union, it's just multiple parts made into one. Currently, it's not that efficient, but that shouldn't stop you from using them. Just only use them when needed.
