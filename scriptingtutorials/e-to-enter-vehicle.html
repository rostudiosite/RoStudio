How to make a e to enter vehicle?

Today we will be creating a e to enter vehicle in Roblox Lua. Throughout this tutorial, you will be learning how to use Proximity Prompts.

Setup:

Inside your vehicle seat create a Proximity Prompt.

Inside the Proximity Prompt create a server script. You can name it whatever you want.

Scripting:

Now we will move on to scripting.

For our variables, we will need the Proximity Prompt and the seat.
